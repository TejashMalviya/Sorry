Our Story

I invited you to a hackathon, and we formed a team. We joined many groups, but the vibe never matched, so we left the groups. But one thing stayed constant — our friendship, you and me.

Our daily walks became the most important routine, even if we had exams the next day, because walks meant more to us than anything else.
